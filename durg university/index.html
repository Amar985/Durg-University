World's Largest University

Durg University is the biggest residential institution in Asia, with over 30,000 students, 18,000 of whom live on campus.
The university is one of the eight public institutions declared as an Institute of Eminence by the Government of India.
Throughout its past and current existence, Durg University has been referred to by a variety of names.
